The Seven Wonders of the World are a group of places around the globe that are considered to be of great importance. These are: Colosseum in Italy., Petra in Jordan , Chichén Itzá in Mexico , Christ the Redeemer in Brazil , Machu Picchu in Peru , Taj Mahal in India and The Great Wall of China.
